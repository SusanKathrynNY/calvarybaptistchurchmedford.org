About This Ministry
Wednesday Bible Study

Every Wednesday evening at 7 PM, Calvary Baptist gathers in the Fellowship Hall for an in-depth study of God's Word. All are welcome — no prior Bible knowledge is required.

Our Wednesday Bible Study is a time of teaching, discussion, and fellowship as we dig deeper into Scripture together. Pastor Chris leads the congregation through books of the Bible and topical studies throughout the year.

What to bring:

Just yourself and your Bible. We provide all study materials. Come as you are — this is a casual, welcoming environment for believers at every stage of their faith journey.
